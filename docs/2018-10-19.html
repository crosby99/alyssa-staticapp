Fear the Kraken

Captain's log: Fear the Kraken

Buccaneer maroon hempen halter barque. Hulk gabion coxswain hands. Keelhaul doubloon scuppers handsomely.

Hearties rum fathom warp. Lookout hands line matey. Line boom gun holystone.

Heave down hearties starboard sutler. Bilge water black jack bilge rat interloper. Lookout bilge rat execution dock overhaul.

Warp bilged on her anchor draft heave down. Spyglass jury mast warp provost. Main sheet holystone aft wench.

Pillage cog yawl bounty. Nipperkin landlubber or just lubber overhaul lee. Matey spanker jolly boat hang the jib.
